# Gold Exchange Benchmark Notes - 2026-04-25

This note records public-reference findings used to improve the KCG site. It is not a claim that KCG is affiliated with any listed organization.

## Sources Checked

- Korea Gold Exchange official site: live international spot prices, domestic prices in KRW/3.75g, market news, and price information are prominent first-screen concerns.
- KRX official gold market guide: KRX Gold Market is an exchange-traded market accessed through securities-company gold spot accounts. KRX explicitly warns that private "gold exchange" style homepage, pre-deposit, reading-room, and OTC physical transactions are unrelated to the KRX Gold Market.
- Korea Standard Gold Exchange: trade method guidance covers homepage, mobile, phone, and in-person flow; the public guide emphasizes order confirmation, phone support, and 1g units.
- Samsung Gold Exchange official mall: product listings, customer service hours, bank/company details, and ecommerce trust information are presented alongside products.
- Gyeongshin Gold Exchange: public pages emphasize branch guidance, real-time prices, buy/sell price distinction, Kakao/phone inquiry, product categories, and visit-oriented conversion.

## Applied Design Decisions

- Keep KCG company-posted prices separate from automated market-reference data.
- Make buy/sell basis, announced time, and on-site final confirmation easier to scan.
- Add KRX confusion-prevention copy because private gold exchange services and KRX Gold Market trading are materially different.
- Improve consultation conversion without adding fabricated certifications, reviews, official affiliations, or unverified legal details.
- Add a purpose-based visit checklist for sell-side products, bars, legal/entity cleanup, and price-only inquiries.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy competitor layouts, testimonials, product names, or price claims.
- Do not claim KRX membership, official certification, hallmark status, business registration details, or live execution unless verified by user-provided documents.
- Do not turn preview/search-blocking into public SEO launch until the user explicitly approves production launch and indexing.
