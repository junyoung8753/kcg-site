# Gold Exchange Benchmark Notes - 2026-04-27

This note records public-reference findings used to improve the KCG site. It is not a ranking claim and it is not a claim that KCG is affiliated with any listed organization.

There is no reliable public "official top 10 Korean gold exchanges" list suitable for product decisions. For KCG, the practical benchmark set is a hybrid of market-leading evidence, search-visible official sites, and official KRX safety guidance.

The official site puts consultation phone numbers, domestic 3.75g price lineup, international spot prices, charts, news, products, and branch paths close to the first screen. ## Patterns Worth Using

- Price lineup first: 24K, 18K, 14K, platinum, and silver with buy/sell distinction and date/time.
- Large campaign visual near the first screen: leading Korean gold-exchange sites commonly pair a price surface with a wide seasonal/product banner, and app-first services use a single oversized device/product hero. For KCG, the useful pattern is the large confident visual, not app-store promotion.
- Phone-first conversion: clear phone number near prices and repeated at high-intent points.
- Visit and branch clarity: address, map, hours, and branch/store guidance are trust builders.
- Product scope: gold bars, silver bars, jewelry, baby gifts, commemorative products, and sell/exchange guidance help visitors self-identify.
- Trust proof through practical facts: posted date, quality language, business details, customer center, account/payment caution, and process copy are more useful than vague claims.
- Market context below company price: international spot, charts, news, and KRX education can help, but they must not visually outrank KCG company prices.

## KCG Direction Chosen

KCG should use a hybrid direction:

- Korea Gold Exchange-style price-first hierarchy.
- Samsung/standard-exchange-style trust and product clarity.
- GBK-style single large campaign image energy, converted to phone/visit consultation rather than app download because KCG has no confirmed public app.
- Local office-style phone, visit, and sell-side consultation flow.
- KRX-style confusion prevention and safety language.

KCG should not become:

- A copied ecommerce mall.
- A live trading platform.
- A KRX-like financial exchange.
- A news/content scraper.
- A multi-option design comparison hub.

## Applied Design Decisions

- Keep KCG company-posted prices separate from automatic market-reference data.
- Make buy/sell basis, announced time, and on-site final confirmation easier to scan.
- Keep a large campaign visual in the first home surface, but make it serve the price desk and consultation CTA. Do not copy competitor app-download banners or seasonal product graphics.
- Keep KRX confusion-prevention copy because private gold exchange services and KRX Gold Market trading are materially different.
- Improve consultation conversion without adding fabricated certifications, reviews, official affiliations, or unverified legal details.
- Add a purpose-based visit checklist for sell-side products, bars, legal/entity cleanup, and price-only inquiries.
- Keep `/` as the single public candidate and remove old option routes.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy competitor layouts, testimonials, product names, images, price tables, chart data, news bodies, or price claims.
- Do not claim KRX membership, official certification, hallmark status, business registration details, or live execution unless verified by user-provided documents.
- Do not turn preview/search-blocking into public SEO launch until the user explicitly approves production launch and indexing.
- External APIs, RSS-like feeds, calculators, forms, and chart data must follow `docs/quality/data-source-compliance.md`.
